# Contact 📩

## We'd Love to Hear From You
Whether you're running into a technical issue, have a question about pricing, or just
want to share feedback — the PaperBeam team is here to help. Reach out through any
of the channels below and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

## Contact Options

- 📧 **Email Support** — Best for detailed questions, bug reports, or account issues
- 💬 **Live Chat** — Get instant help directly from the PaperBeam support team
- 🌐 **Community Forum** — Connect with other PaperBeam users, share tips, and browse answers to common questions
- 🐛 **Bug Reports** — Found something broken? Submit a report and our dev team will investigate

## Support Hours

Our team is available during the following hours. Messages received outside of these
times will be answered on the next business day.

1. Monday to Friday
2. 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM EST
3. Response within 24 hours guaranteed
4. Weekend emergency support available for Pro and Enterprise plans

## Which Channel Should I Use?

| Channel | Best For | Response Time |
|---------|----------|---------------|
| Email | Account issues, billing, detailed questions | Within 24 hours |
| Live Chat | Quick troubleshooting and fast answers | Under 5 minutes |
| Community Forum | General discussion and sharing ideas | Community driven |
| Bug Reports | Reporting errors or unexpected behaviour | 1–3 business days |

::: tip Pro Tip
For the fastest resolution, include your workspace name and a brief description
of the issue when reaching out via email or live chat.
:::

::: info Enterprise Support
Enterprise plan users have access to a dedicated account manager and priority
support queue. Contact us to learn more about Enterprise pricing.
:::
