# Products 🛠️

## What is PaperBeam?
PaperBeam is a modern content publishing platform built for teams who take their
writing seriously. From solo bloggers to large editorial teams, PaperBeam adapts
to your workflow — keeping everything organized, collaborative, and fast.

## Core Features

- 📐 **Flexible Content Layouts** — Build pages that look exactly how you want, with Flextext handling the structure automatically
- ⚡ **Fast Editing Workflow** — No bloated interfaces. Just open a file, write, and save. Changes reflect instantly.
- 🤝 **Shared Publishing Tools** — Invite teammates, assign roles, and publish together without stepping on each other's work
- 🔍 **Built-in Search** — Every page is indexed automatically so your readers can always find what they're looking for
- 📊 **Content Analytics** — Track which pages get the most attention and use that data to improve your content strategy
